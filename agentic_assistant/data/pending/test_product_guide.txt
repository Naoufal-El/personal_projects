Product Guide - Customer Service Knowledge Base

Welcome to our comprehensive product guide. This document contains essential information about our products and services to help customer service representatives answer common questions.

Product Categories

Electronics
Our electronics category includes smartphones, tablets, laptops, and accessories. All electronic items come with a standard 1-year warranty. Extended warranty options are available for purchase at checkout.

Home Appliances
We offer a wide range of home appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, and microwaves. Most appliances include free installation within 50 miles of our service centers.

Furniture
Our furniture collection features living room, bedroom, and office furniture. All furniture items are available with customizable fabric and color options. Delivery and assembly services are included for orders over $500.

Common Questions

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most items. The product must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Electronics and appliances require original packaging for returns.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping options are available for an additional fee.

Q: Do you offer price matching?
A: Yes, we match prices from authorized retailers. You must provide proof of the competitor's price within 7 days of your purchase.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and financing options through our partner providers.

Warranty Information

Standard Warranty
All products include a manufacturer's warranty. Warranty periods vary by product category:
- Electronics: 1 year
- Appliances: 2 years
- Furniture: 5 years

Extended Protection Plans
Extended protection plans are available for purchase and cover accidental damage, mechanical failures, and normal wear and tear beyond the manufacturer's warranty period.
